Here are a some interests of mine


Music
I love music! I play Bass Guitar and Bari Saxophone, in small groups and community bands. The last group I played in was the Beethoven German Band in San Antonio TX. I have worked for two music stores, Kennelly Keys Music in Lynnwood WA, and Matanuska Music in Wasilla AK.



Food
There is so much great food in Seattle! With so many great choices, it can hard to pick. There are many good restaurants with a wide verity of foods. Being in the northwest, the Seattle area has access to great seafood, amazing local produce from around Washington, and locally sourced meats. Going out for a culinary adventure or staying home to cook awesome northwest ingredients are both great options and I very much like them both.
